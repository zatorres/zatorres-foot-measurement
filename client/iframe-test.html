Zatorres Size Guide

Dedicated sizing page for Shopify integration

Integration Information

  • URL: http://localhost:3000 (for development) or your production URL
  • Responsive: The app is fully responsive and works on all device sizes
  • Features: Dual foot measurement, last selection, detailed recommendations
  • Branding: Uses Zatorres color scheme and luxury styling
  • Contact: Includes email and phone options for complimentary sizing consultation

Shopify Integration Notes

  • Dedicated Page: Perfect for a "Size Guide" or "Find Your Size" page
  • Iframe Embedding: Ready for direct iframe integration into Shopify
  • Mobile Optimized: Works seamlessly on Shopify mobile themes
  • Brand Consistent: Maintains Zatorres luxury aesthetic
  • Customer Journey: Helps customers who don't know their size find the perfect fit
